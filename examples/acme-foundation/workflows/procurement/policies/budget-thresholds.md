# Budget Approval Thresholds

## Purpose

This policy defines the tiered approval thresholds for procurement expenditures
at the Acme Foundation. These thresholds ensure appropriate oversight
proportional to the financial commitment while maintaining operational
efficiency for routine purchases.

## Approval Tiers

### Tier 1: $0 - $5,000 (Routine Purchases)

- **Approver:** Procurement Lead
- **Requirements:** Standard purchase request form, single vendor quote
  acceptable for purchases under $1,000. Two quotes required for $1,000-$5,000.
- **Timeline:** Approval within 2 business days of complete submission.

### Tier 2: $5,001 - $25,000 (Significant Purchases)

- **Approver:** Finance Director
- **Requirements:** Minimum of three competitive bids, completed vendor
  assessment report, budget availability confirmation from SAP, and written
  justification linking the purchase to an approved program or operational need.
- **Timeline:** Approval within 5 business days of complete submission.

### Tier 3: $25,001 - $100,000 (Major Purchases)

- **Approver:** Finance Director with Board notification
- **Requirements:** All Tier 2 requirements plus a formal procurement memo
  summarizing the competitive process, a cost-benefit analysis, and
  confirmation that the expenditure is within the Board-approved annual budget.
  The Board Finance Committee must be notified within 5 business days of
  approval.
- **Timeline:** Approval within 10 business days of complete submission.

### Tier 4: Over $100,000 (Capital and Strategic Purchases)

- **Approver:** Board of Directors (majority vote)
- **Requirements:** All Tier 3 requirements plus a formal presentation to the
  Board, legal review of the proposed contract, and a multi-year financial
  impact analysis if the commitment extends beyond the current fiscal year.
- **Timeline:** Must be submitted at least 15 business days before the next
  scheduled Board meeting. Emergency Board sessions may be convened for
  time-sensitive procurement per the Foundation's bylaws.

## Aggregate Spending Controls

Multiple purchases from the same vendor within a 90-day period that
collectively exceed a tier threshold must be approved at the higher tier level.
This prevents circumvention of approval thresholds through order splitting.

## Budget Availability

No purchase may be approved unless sufficient funds are available in the
designated budget line item. The Finance Director must verify budget
availability in SAP before approving any Tier 2 or higher expenditure.

## Fiscal Year Considerations

Purchases that span fiscal years must be approved based on the total contract
value, not the current-year portion alone. Multi-year commitments over $25,000
require Board awareness regardless of the annual expenditure amount.
