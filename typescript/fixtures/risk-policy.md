# Vendor Risk Assessment Policy

## Risk Categories

### Low Risk
- Annual contract value under $500K
- Vendor has SOC 2 Type II or equivalent certification
- No compliance violations in the past 3 years
- Operating history of 5+ years
- Jurisdiction with strong regulatory framework (US, EU, UK, Canada, Australia)

### Medium Risk
- Annual contract value between $500K and $2M
- Vendor has SOC 2 Type I but not Type II
- Minor compliance violations (resolved) in the past 3 years
- Operating history of 2-5 years
- Missing one or more preferred certifications

### High Risk
- Annual contract value over $2M
- No SOC 2 certification
- Active or unresolved compliance violations
- Operating history under 2 years
- Jurisdiction with limited regulatory oversight
- Vendor handles PII or sensitive financial data without ISO 27001

## Required Actions by Risk Level

### Low Risk
- Standard due diligence review
- Annual compliance check
- Notify compliance team (informational)

### Medium Risk
- Enhanced due diligence review
- Semi-annual compliance check
- Compliance team review required before approval
- Require remediation plan for any gaps

### High Risk
- Full due diligence investigation
- Quarterly compliance monitoring
- Senior compliance officer approval required
- Legal review of contract terms
- Consider additional insurance requirements

## Approval Authority

- Low Risk: Department manager may approve
- Medium Risk: Compliance team must review; director-level approval
- High Risk: Senior compliance officer + VP approval required
